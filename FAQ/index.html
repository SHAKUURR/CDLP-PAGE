FAQs

This are the common questions asked by new and existing users

How many brands are under CDLP HUB LIMITED?

We have six active brands accommodated by CDLP HUB LIMITED.

How can I purchase airtime or data?

You have to login into TopItUpNg.com, register, activate your account, fund your wallet and you can buy airtime or data for your network.

What is Automation all about?

It is a platform that automatically credit your network with data or airtime without dialing *000#, hence, you need to own a data and airtime website before you can automate. 

Is the automation platform free?

No, it has a monthly/yearly subscription. Learn more via the website www.simserver.io

How does Bulk SMS work?

Via www.PMCSMS.com you can register, create your SENDER ID that your contact will recognise that it’s you, fund your wallet and get ready to start sending the bulk SMS. 

How many days or weeks will it take to deliver website/mobile app development? 

Depending on the services to develop but a minimum of 4 weeks as an airtime and data website takes just 24hrs. 

Who can I reach if I am having difficulty in setting up any account or need more understanding? 

Our customer Care Support Team is 24/7 available to attend to you but very active from 8am-5pm African time. 

Can I schedule a Demo? 

Yes, you can. Our Customer Care Support is up for the task. 

What do I need to register on any platform? 

Please, make sure you have a GMAIL account for authentication and verification during your registration. Goodluck! 

Do I need a website to sell bulk data too? 

No, you can sell bulk data without website ownership. Get started Now, visit www.datashareportal.com
